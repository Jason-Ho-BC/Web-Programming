Description

Vancouver has a scenic view from it's harbour and home of friendly people. Vancouver is known around the world as a popular tourist attraction and, is also home to the 2010 Winter Olympics. Blessed by mother nature, Vancouver has temperate rain forests, wildlife, and beautiful mountains.
